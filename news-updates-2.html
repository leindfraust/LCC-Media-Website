MONTH OF NUTRITION

Night Mode


“UGALING MAGTANIM, SAPAT NA NUTRISYON AANIHIN” it was another ecstatic day to all LCCians for this month’s anticipated occurrence has finally commenced. A month brimful of various activities that surely did help stimulate and expose the prodigious talents of each expanding individuals. This theme attains to increase the awareness of Filipinos on pressing nutrition concerns, to contribute to improve food and nutrition safety by increasing access to a diversified source of food for families and communities, additionally, to encapsulate the message of food gardening. This event was gladly headed by our very own, Ms. Maria Malou Alcanse, together with the Home Economics Club Members, aided by the Student Government Council and their mentor Mr. Miguel Salvania. Despite the heavy rain and murky weather, the celebration went on, participants were as lively as the wind. In celebration with the wholesome foods, a team composed of 3-5 members in each section from each year level engaged in the Cooking Competition with their main ingredient, coconut milk better known as “gata”. A bunch of pupils from the Elementary Department also got involved in the Blending Contest. They were gaily doing their thing. A few chosen students from each section in every grade level and department hurriedly went up to their designated spots, prepared their art supplies for the Poster Making Contest. An Extemporaneous Speaking Contest took place at the stage, followed by a Jingle Contest that became the most conspicuous among the rest. An Essay Writing Contest was also held at the BED Library. The quadrangle was bombarded with laughter and smiles and deafening applause that surely helped pumped every student in the campus. To end the program, a brief message was delivered by the Home Economics moderator that brought warmth and delight in our hearts. Awarding of winners was the day after tomorrow, everyone went home full of interrogatives and excitement. Another successful affair indeed!
